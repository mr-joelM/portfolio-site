About me

Becoming a software developer

I was employed by Emirates Airline for over 12 years until I was made redundant due to the economic climate created by covid-19 virus.

I took this as an opportunity for a change of career to software development as it is something that I always aspired to, but never took the leap.

I took part in a full time 14 weeks coding bootcamp with Northcoders where I graduated. I was introduced to every aspect of full-stack development, from the fundamentals to the back end where we built our own servers, to the front end using React and deploying on Netlify.

I have since taken a position as a Junior UI Front End Developer in a small digital agency. I have been making numerous bespoke websites using HTML, CSS, javascript, bootstrap, jquery and some Vue.js.

I really enjoy my position in this company and I've learned a lot. However working as a civil servant is something that I've been aiming towards since I started coding, but the opportunity never presented itself at the right time until now.

I am a creative individual eager to learn and progress, who can perform enthusiastically as part of a team as well as solo.
